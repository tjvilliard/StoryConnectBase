Contradictions:
- Lord Henry Wotton is described as smoking cigarettes in the old text, but it is stated in the new text that he has never smoked.
- Dorian Gray is described as a young man of extraordinary personal beauty in the old text, but in the new text, he is described as old and decrepit.
- The garden is described as having trees that emit the scent of lilac and pink-flowering thorn in the old text, but in the new text, it is described as barren.
